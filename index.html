WELCOME TO YOUTHPRINUA

Community work & Library

After seeing the hardship africa kids are going through to access books and other learning materials in our COMMUNITY leading to some even droping school.

We formed organization consisting of different youth groups to come up with a very good solution that would help our upcoming kids by building a community library. Though we experince some hardship like getting books and other learning materials that would be helpful incase needed. We would urge any individual visiting this site to please support by donating either financially or by giving us books!
